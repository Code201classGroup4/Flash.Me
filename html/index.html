Get Started

  • To get started, first login using your name. If you've been here before, don't worry we remember you.
  • Once you're logged on, head on over to Create new Card to create a new flash card. If you've been here before, and don't need to add any new cards to any decks, choose a deck from below and head over to Flash Yourself.
  • In Flash Yourself is where your study session begins. Take your time and consider your choices, select your answer, and move on to the next card. Once you've finished a session, follow the prompt to view your results.
  • In results is where you can view your cummulative results
